About Me

Hi, I'm Sebastian, a rising sophomore at Whitman College in Walla Walla, Washington. I'm currently double majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics, and strongly considering a Philosophy degree as well! I'm interested in examining ways people interact with each other, how digital tools help or hurt our learning and socialization, and looking at how we teach math and computer science to people of all ages.

I am also interested in algorithms, games, and using math (and by extension computer science) to solve weird, fun problems.

This website showcases my work, thoughts, and projects. Feel free to explore and get in touch with me through the contact information provided.
